WE ARE MMT

Rely on MMT for world-class equipment and machinery and dedicated after sales support to take your projects forward.

A leading supplier of construction equipment in the UAE, Modern Machinery Trading LLC has more than 25 years of experience in the construction industry. With a significant stint in the construction industry, serving clientele stretching across Construction, Oil and Gas, Agriculture and Landscaping, we pride ourselves on being a major contributor to the development of the UAE. Playing the dual role as a supplier and stockist we help our clients obtain equipment and spare parts at the right time, every single time.
